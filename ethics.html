General IT Ethics

Here are some simple IT related guidelines that should be prioritzed in the modern day based off the ACM Code of Ethics:

Developers should actively be trying to work towards the good of society and avoid harm.

The goal should always be to avoid harm as much as possible and be transparent and hoenst with the users.

Privacy is uptmost importance and any actions done to undermine or profit off user's private information is immoral.

Assocation for Computing Machinery. (2025). "Code of Ethics" https://www.acm.org/code-of-ethics.
