General IT Ethics

Here are some important IT-related guidelines that should be prioritized in the modern day, based on the ACM Code of Ethics:

  • Developers should work actively toward the good of society and avoid harm.
  • Efforts should be made to minimize harm as much as possible and be transparent and honest with users.
  • Privacy must be treated as a top priority. Undermining or profiting from users' private information is considered unethical.

Reference

Association for Computing Machinery. (2018). ACM Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct. Retrieved from https://www.acm.org/code-of-ethics.
